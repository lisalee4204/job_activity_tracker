# Demo Data Guide

## What I Added

I've added a **"Load Demo Data"** button that appears on your dashboard when you have no activities. This will populate your account with 60+ sample job search activities.

## Features

✅ **60+ Sample Activities** - Spread across last 8 weeks
✅ **Various Companies** - Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Apple, etc.
✅ **Different Job Titles** - Software Engineer, Product Manager, etc.
✅ **Multiple Activity Types** - Applications, interviews, networking, etc.
✅ **Various Statuses** - Application, HR Screen, Final Round, Offer, Rejected
✅ **Different Sources** - LinkedIn, Indeed, Company Website, etc.
✅ **Current Week Activities** - Ensures weekly summary shows data

## How to Use

1. **Sign in** to your account
2. If you have no activities, you'll see a **"Load Demo Data"** button
3. Click it to populate your dashboard with sample data
4. See visualizations, analytics, and weekly summaries populate!

## What You'll See

After loading demo data:
- ✅ **Activities Table** - 60+ activities with various companies and roles
- ✅ **Weekly Summary** - Shows progress across multiple weeks
- ✅ **Analytics** - Top companies, job titles, activity types
- ✅ **Goal Tracking** - See how weekly goals are met
- ✅ **Charts** - Visualizations of your job search activity

## Reset Demo Data

To clear demo data and start fresh:
- Delete activities manually, or
- The demo data function will replace existing data when run again

## Technical Details

- Demo data is seeded via database function
- Activities are randomly distributed across last 8 weeks
- Includes realistic variety in companies, titles, and statuses
- Current week always has activities for immediate feedback

Enjoy exploring your app with demo data! 🎉
